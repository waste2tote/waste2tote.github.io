About Waste2Tote

Every year, over 300 million tonnes of plastic are produced worldwide. At least 8 million tonnes pollute oceans, harming wildlife and the environment.

Waste2Tote collects plastic wrappers and packaging and upcycles them into durable, reusable tote bags. We combine creativity with community action to reduce plastic waste and empower people.

Reuse. Rethink. Rebuild.

Our Mission

To reduce plastic pollution by transforming waste into functional, eco-friendly tote bags while educating and engaging communities in sustainable practices.

  • Collect plastic waste responsibly
  • Empower communities through training
  • Promote sustainable alternatives
