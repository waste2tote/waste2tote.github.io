About the Initiative

Every year, over 300 million tonnes of plastic are produced globally—and at least 8 million tonnes end up in our oceans. Often non‑biodegradable, these items choke wildlife, pollute, and exacerbate the climate crisis.

At Waste2Tote, we flip the script. We collect plastic waste—wrappers, bags, packaging—and turn them into durable tote bags. Through creative upcycling and community engagement, we give waste a second life—and spread the message that change starts with us.

Reuse. Rethink. Rebuild.
